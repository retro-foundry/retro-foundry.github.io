For more projects use the top-right links (GitHub, Discord, or Patreon).

Mouse: The pointer is visible until you left-click in the window to capture it (windowed or fullscreen). Press Esc once to release capture

Move: WASD or arrow keys; mouse turns when captured. Shift: run; C: duck; L: look behind. Fire: mouse or Alt/Ctrl; use: Space or right mouse when captured.

Weapons: 1–5 (or numpad); mouse wheel cycles. Tab: automap; PgUp/PgDn zoom. F5/F9 quick save/load. Save games will not currently persist between sessions.

Web build: After you finish level 3, the game returns to level 1, this is a 3 level demo. F11 toggles fullscreen.

Audio may stay muted until you click or tap the page (browser autoplay policy).
